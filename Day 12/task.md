# 📢 Innolift Ventures Internship – Day 12 Task

## Technical Case Study and Domain Research

Hello Interns! 👋

Yesterday, you completed the basic case study for your assigned project. Today, the focus is not on features or modules, but on gaining a deeper technical understanding of your project domain and the technologies required to build it.

---

# 🎯 Objective

Develop a strong technical foundation for your final project by studying:

1. Your project domain in detail.
2. The technologies required to implement the project.
3. Existing solutions and technical architectures used in the industry.

This task will help you understand **how your project actually works technically** before beginning full implementation.

---

# 📌 Part 1 – Domain Research

Prepare a detailed study about your project domain.

Your report should include:

## 1. Domain Introduction

* What is the domain?
* Why is this domain important?
* Current industry trends.

---

## 2. Industry Problems

Explain:

* Current challenges faced in this domain.
* Why existing systems are inefficient.
* Technical limitations of current solutions.

---

## 3. Existing Solutions Analysis

Research at least **3 existing applications/products** related to your project.

For each product, study:

* Company/Product Name
* Features
* Technologies Used
* Advantages
* Limitations

---

## 4. Market Research

Study:

* Target users
* Target industries
* Future demand
* Growth opportunities
